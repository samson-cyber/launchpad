LaunchPad — Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 28, 2026

Data Collection

LaunchPad does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. No analytics, telemetry, or usage data is gathered.

Local Storage

All shortcuts and settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. This data never leaves your browser.

Third-Party Services

LaunchPad does not communicate with any third-party servers or services.

Tracking

There are no cookies, pixels, fingerprinting, or any other form of tracking.

Account

No account or sign-in is required to use LaunchPad.

Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
